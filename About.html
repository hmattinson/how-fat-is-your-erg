How FAT is your Ergo?

Formula taken from Concept website:

Power = 2.8/(pace*pace*pace)

where pace is time in seconds over distance in metres.

Weight Factor = (Weight in pounds / 270) to the power of 0.222

Corected Time = Weight Factor * Time

Corrected Distance = Distance / Weight Factor

Classifying FATNESS

I wanted a way to take into account weight,power and distance to create one metric that I could then test against a baseline metric. The problem initially was that over shorter distance any rower will put out more power. To take this into account I made the metric:

α = (Power/Weight Factor)*Distance^β

Where β is 0.217. This was worked out by finding the value that would minimise the deviation α for a variety of mens' records. Deviation was still moderately high but when ording events by α the order of the distance/time of the events seemed suitably random.
